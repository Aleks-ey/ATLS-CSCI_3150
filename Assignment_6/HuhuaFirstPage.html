Welcome to my first webpage. While you are here, let me tell you about Elden Ring. Elden Ring is the newest game in a series of "souls-like" games created by FromSoftware. It is an action role-playing game centered around a distinct style of gameplay that challenges the player. In my opinion, Elden Ring is the greatest game ever made. The story for Elden Ring was even written in collaboration with George R. R. Martin, the creator of Game of Thrones.
